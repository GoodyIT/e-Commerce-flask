About Us

Eloraus is an international B2C fast esports fashion e-commerce platform. The company mainly focuses on esports, but it also offers other accessories, shoes, bags and other fashion items. Eloraus mainly targets Europe, America, Australia, and the Middle East along with other consumer markets. The brand was founded in October 2017, and since then it has upheld the philosophy that "everyone can enjoy esports."
